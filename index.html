Some Of The Freuently Asked Questions

What is the cost of building a new website?

The exact cost of building a new website depends on different factors. Such as the type of tech stack the developers use, the complexity the project, and some resources costs.

So, it is difficult to predict an average cost for web development. However you can discuss the the project guideline and expectations with Genius Techub and we will provide you with an estimated cost after careful evaluation.

Is there an e-commerce website maintenance and support service available under Genius Techub?

Yes, the experts at our agency do extend high-grade e-commerce website development and upgradation support. Our specialists offer consistent and thorough support and maintenance work during web development processes. Expect cost-effective rates.

Do you redesign existing website?

Yes. We can redesign existing sites and retain your organization's style or give you a completely new look. Genius Techub can also provide a free website evaluation. We will show you how your site can take advantage of the latest Web technologies.

We are also happy to look at your existing site and give you a quote for updating it.

What is the overall time to complete website development?

The time it takes to complete a web development project depends on the type of website being made, level of customization, and development skillset. To note, it takes around 1-4 months approximately to create standard-level websites under a web development company, in general.

If you have a deadline, we will work hard to meet it. The most common cause of delay is the content (text/images) from the client. Please complete the discussion about the potential deadline during the first meeting for best results.

What pricing model you propose to your clients & when do they pay?

If the project scope is fixed then we provide fixed cost and timeline estimates. For ongoing maintenance, upgrade and ongoing developments we propose our clients to hire our developers on hourly or fixed monthly basis on affordable costs.

We offer a variety of payment options, varying from a one-time, up-front payment. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible.

What if I need help on my site down the road?

We are only an email away! We are here to help you as much or as little as you need, and we won't disappear once the site is launched. We have been doing this for years, so we aren't going anywhere anytime soon.
